Title: "The Guardian of Dreams"
(Right to protection )

In a world not unlike ours, there was a magical land known as Dreamlandia. In Dreamlandia, children's dreams were as precious as the brightest stars in the night sky, and there was a special right called the "Right to Protection." Meet Sarah, a 14-year-old girl who lived in Dreamlandia. Sarah had a unique gift - she could see and feel the dreams of others. Every night, as she closed her eyes, she would visit Dreamlandia and explore the dreamscape.


One day, Sarah met a young boy named Finn in Dreamlandia. Finn's dream was filled with wonder and adventure, but it was being threatened by dark, shadowy figures. Sarah knew it was her duty to protect Finn's dream. She remembered her grandmother's words: "Sarah, in Dreamlandia, every child has the right to dream without fear. It's your job to be their guardian." Sarah decided to learn more about the Right to Protection. She embarked on a journey through Dreamlandia, seeking the wisdom of the Dreamkeepers, ancient creatures who watched over children's dreams.


The Dreamkeepers told her, "The Right to Protection means that every child has the right to dream in peace. No one should ever feel scared or harmed in their dreams." Armed with this knowledge, Sarah returned to Finn's dream. With bravery in her heart, she faced the shadowy figures and said, "You can't harm this dream. Finn has the Right to Protection, and I'll defend it!" The shadows tried to intimidate her, but Sarah stood her ground, knowing that she had the power to protect dreams. She called upon the Dreamkeepers, who surrounded her with a warm, protective light.


he shadows vanished, and Finn's dream became safe and full of happiness once more. Finn woke up with a smile, feeling the warmth of Sarah's protection. As Sarah continued her adventures in Dreamlandia, she realized that being a guardian of dreams was a noble responsibility. She made it her mission to ensure that all children enjoyed their Right to Protection in their dreams, just like Finn. And so, in Dreamlandia, children slept soundly, knowing that Sarah and the Dreamkeepers would always be there to protect their dreams, making sure they were free to dream big and dream without fear.
